Legacy

Jose Rizal's legacy wasn't that big to other people. But for us Filipinos, he left a big legacy for us. He was considered as a national hero of the Philippine Country. Jose was remembered by his brilliance and his Filipino peace, courage and also his intelligence. Jose was a great writer too, he dedicated his life for the peace of the Filipino country. Schools now study his life, his poems and also his legacy. He wrote 2 famous novels and wrote lots of beautiful poems. While Jose was at Spain, he wrote about Spain ruling his country, Phillippines. He went back to Phillippines at the year 1892 but died at the day December 30, 1896 because he was convicted as sedition. Even though he supported peaceful change but never accomplished it, he did made a change to the country, Philippines. People knew him as number one hero of the Phillipines. He was clever because he was the only one whose to fight for his loving country, Phillipines. He wanted freedom and indepence for his country.
